# Messaging Guidelines

## Voice

- **Confident**, not arrogant
- **Executive-friendly** — lead with business impact
- **Precise** — specific outcomes over vague superlatives
- **Evidence-based** — metrics and proof when available

## Outcomes-first formula

```
[Outcome] + [for whom] + [how we deliver] + [proof optional]
```

Examples:

- "Reduce time-to-market for enterprise SaaS by combining AI consulting with a unified delivery framework."
- "Cut operational overhead with automated cloud pipelines and multi-agent AI workflows."

## Words to prefer

- Outcomes: reduce, accelerate, scale, automate, de-risk, optimize
- Trust: proven, measured, enterprise-grade, auditable, compliant
- Partnership: long-term, embedded, dedicated, accountable

## Words to avoid on marketing pages

- "Best-in-class", "world-class", "cutting-edge" (without proof)
- Technology laundry lists without business context
- "We build websites/apps" (too generic)

## Page-level messaging

| Page type | Lead with |
|-----------|-----------|
| Home | Combined value prop: consulting + product |
| Services | Business problems solved |
| Product | Team productivity outcomes |
| Case study | Client challenge → measurable result |
| Pricing | Engagement model clarity + ROI framing |
| Contact | Response time + what happens next |

## CTAs

Match intent:

| Visitor stage | CTA |
|---------------|-----|
| Awareness | View Case Studies, Explore Services |
| Consideration | Book Discovery Call, Download Overview |
| Decision | Contact Sales, Start Free Trial |
